💧 Drink Water

Drink 8 to 10 glasses of water every day. Water helps control blood sugar. Always keep a water bottle with you.

👣 Check Your Feet

Look at your feet every day. Check for cuts, blisters, or red spots. Diabetes can make feet feel numb. Use a mirror to see the bottom.

🏃 Walk Every Day

Walk for 30 minutes daily. Walking helps control blood sugar. Start slowly, then walk faster. It is good for your heart too.

🍎 Eat Healthy Food

Eat vegetables, fruits, and whole grains. Eat at regular times. Do not skip meals. This helps keep blood sugar steady.

😌 Relax Your Mind

Stress increases blood sugar. Take deep breaths. Sit quietly for 5 minutes. Listen to music. Sleep well at night.

💊 Take Medicine on Time

Take your medicine at the same time every day. Set a reminder on your phone. Do not miss doses. Ask your doctor if you have questions.
