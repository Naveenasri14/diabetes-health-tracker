📖 Myth vs Fact

Separate diabetes myths from facts

❌ MYTH

Diabetes is curable

✅ FACT

Diabetes is a chronic condition that can be managed but not cured

📝 Explanation: While there's no cure for diabetes, it can be well managed through diet, exercise, and medication. Many people with diabetes live healthy, active lives.

❌ MYTH

Eating sugar causes diabetes

✅ FACT

Sugar itself doesn't cause diabetes

📝 Explanation: Type 1 diabetes is autoimmune. Type 2 is related to genetics, lifestyle factors, and overall diet quality - not just sugar consumption.

❌ MYTH

Diabetics cannot eat fruits

✅ FACT

Fruits are healthy and can be eaten in moderation

📝 Explanation: Whole fruits contain fiber and nutrients. Choose whole fruits over juice, limit portions, and pair with protein for better blood sugar control.

❌ MYTH

Insulin cures diabetes

✅ FACT

Insulin manages diabetes, it does not cure it

📝 Explanation: Insulin helps control blood sugar but diabetes is a chronic condition that requires ongoing management throughout life.

❌ MYTH

Only overweight people get diabetes

✅ FACT

Anyone can develop diabetes, regardless of weight

📝 Explanation: Type 1 diabetes is autoimmune. Type 2 has genetic factors too. Weight is only one of many risk factors.
